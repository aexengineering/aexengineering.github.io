About Company


The company was founded in 2016 by Massimiliano Zonta.
The identity of the company, vision, mission and values are completely expressed by the motto "Automating user experience to enhance production quality".


Vision, Mission and Values


VISION
Use automation to enhance the production quality leveraging on culture and values of the customer.

MISSION
Design automation systems that realize a good tradeoff among the constraints:
  • Customer needs and experience
  • Customer policies on management, maintenance and development of the production process
  • Open to interface with other systems and devices
  • Open to technology trends

VALUES
  • Work to create long-term relationship with customer and collaborators
  • Attention at the details to create good solutions and good products
