About Company


AEX Engineering is the company founded by Massimiliano Zonta.
The identity of the company, vision, mission and values are completely expressed by the motto "Automating user experience to enhance production quality". This is the synthesis of the professioinal experince that the owner has developed in the past years of activity while he worked making automation software for processing industry. This expressed completely the way the company works everyday.
And these are the principles that will guide the company in the research of collaborators.


Vision, Mission and Values


VISION: Use automation to enhance the production quality leveraging on customer culture and values.

MISSION: Design automation systems that make a good trade-off between the constraints:
  • Customer needs and experience
  • Customer policy on process, maintenance and development
  • Open to interface with other systems and devices
  • Open to technology trends

VALUES:
  • Work to create long-term relationship with customer and collaborators
  • Attention at the details to create good solutions and products
